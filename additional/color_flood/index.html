Color Flood Game

Clicks: 0

Game Rules:

  1. The goal is to make the entire board the same color as the top-left square.
  2. Click on a square adjacent to the connected squares to change their color.
  3. Connected squares are those that share a color with the top-left square and are directly or indirectly adjacent to it.
  4. Only adjacent squares can be clicked; clicking a non-adjacent square does not count as a turn and nothing will happen.
  5. Keep track of the number of clicks it takes to complete the game!
